FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about EliteLoop.

What is EliteLoop?

EliteLoop is an activity matching platform designed for distinguished individuals. It helps people meet through shared interests and special events while keeping privacy central.

How does the badge system work?

There are 3 badge levels: Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Bronze is free. Silver and Gold unlock more visibility, access, and messaging power.

How is privacy ensured?

Your location is approximate, not exact. Personal information is protected with Apple Sign-In and controlled profile sharing.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from App Store settings. Premium stays active until the end of the current billing period.

Which countries is it available in?

EliteLoop supports Turkish, English, Russian, Arabic, Chinese, and Japanese in its product direction and is designed for a global community.

Is EliteLoop available on Android?

Yes! EliteLoop is now live on both the App Store and Google Play. Download it on any iOS or Android device.
