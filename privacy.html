Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

EliteLoop ("we", "our", or "Company") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the EliteLoop mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address (via Apple Sign-In)
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, interests, languages, bio
  • Location Information: Location data to show nearby events and users
  • User Content: Events you create, messages you send
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status (processed by Apple)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show nearby events and users
  • To personalize your app experience
  • To enable messaging between users
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers (cloud storage, payment processors)
  • When required by law

4. Location Privacy

Your location is only collected when you grant permission. Your approximate location (fuzzy) is shown to other users, but your exact address is never shared. You can disable location sharing at any time from Settings.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Data portability

To delete your account, go to Settings > Delete Account in the App.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies in our mobile app. We do not track users across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced via in-app notification.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions:

Email: support@eliteloop.app

Website: https://eliteloop.app
