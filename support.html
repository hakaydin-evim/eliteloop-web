Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my account verified with a Member Badge?

EliteLoop utilizes a Badge system (Bronze, Silver, Gold) to govern event access and visibility. You automatically receive a Bronze badge upon joining for free. You can instantly upgrade to Silver or Gold via an in-app subscription in your Profile settings to unlock higher-tier VIP events, direct messaging capabilities, and full member profile visibility.

I switched phones, how do I restore my EliteLoop access?

If you lost access to your primary login method, please email info@eliteloop.app from your registered billing email address so our concierge team can assist.

Can I hide my profile from search completely?

EliteLoop operates under absolute discretion. Your profile is already invisible to standard users utilizing "Stealth Mode" networking algorithms. You are only suggested to individuals matching your immediate trust tier or professional filters.

How do I cancel my subscription?

All subscriptions are seamlessly managed through your platform provider. You can cancel your subscription at any time via your Apple App Store or Google Play Store settings.
